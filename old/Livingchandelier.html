Living Chandelier

Indoor hydroponic garden



Year: 2016



The goal of this project was to improve our daily experience of kitchen by enabling people to farm in their kitchen!

The modern lifestyles and living spaces have made it harder than before to keep the habit of kitchen–gardening and benefit from all its perks like air purification, access to organic foods, cutting transportation costs, etc.

Living Chandelier is my attempt to bring back the sense of organicity and freshness into our small kitchens by designing a compact hydroponic system suitable for an indoor setting.
